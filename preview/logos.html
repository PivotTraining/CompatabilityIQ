CompatibleIQ Logo Concepts

Five distinct logo directions for the CompatibleIQ psychometric dating platform. Scientific but warm, premium but accessible.

Purple #7B68B5
Green #4CAF8A
Coral #E8735A
Concept 1
CIQ Monogram
A clean, geometric CIQ letterform inside a rounded square. Works as an app icon, favicon, or standalone brand mark. The green dot on the "i" adds a subtle accent.
Concept 2
Connection
Two interlocking rings representing compatibility and connection. The overlap zone with coral accent dots highlights the intersection between two people. The gradient connection suggests a science-driven link.
Concept 3
Pulse
A heartbeat/pulse line that peaks into a subtle heart shape with brain-like arcs above it. The line connects two nodes, symbolizing the scientific measurement of romantic connection.
Concept 4
Spectrum
A compatibility score meter integrated beneath the wordmark. The gradient spectrum runs from coral through purple to green, with a score indicator showing "87%." Communicates the data-driven nature of the product.
Concept 5
Minimal
An ultra-clean wordmark with a diamond accent replacing the dot on the "i" and "IQ" set in the brand purple at a heavier weight. A green dot punctuates the end. Less is more.
